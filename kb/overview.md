# AminQa Solutions – Company Overview

AminQa Solutions is a Pakistan-based technology engineering company specializing in **AI-powered CRM systems, enterprise-grade customer platforms, cloud infrastructure, and data engineering**. Established with a mission to transform businesses into modern digital-first organizations, AminQa delivers advanced, scalable, and secure solutions to clients across multiple industries and countries.

The company operates from its on-site office located at:

**201, 1st Floor, Ashraf Commercial Center,  
Near Allama Iqbal Avenue, Dewan Puli,  
Bahawalpur, Punjab, Pakistan.**

AminQa combines deep engineering expertise, global delivery experience, and modern AI technologies to help organizations streamline workflows, automate operations, and make data-driven decisions.

# Company Evolution

AminQa began as a **remote and outsourcing-based engineering practice**, working with developers and clients across borders. The company delivered:

- AI-enabled CRMs  
- Business automation systems  
- Enterprise dashboards  
- Cloud infrastructure deployments  
- Data analytics pipelines  
- Custom system modules  

In **November 2025**, AminQa transitioned into a **full on-site office** in Bahawalpur to streamline delivery, create a cohesive engineering culture, and expand internal capabilities.
