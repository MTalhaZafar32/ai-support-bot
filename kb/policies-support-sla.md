# Support Policy & SLA – AminQa Solutions

This document describes the support process and service-level commitments.

## Support Channels
- Email support  
- Client WhatsApp groups  
- Ticketing system  
- Scheduled calls  

---

# Response Times

| Issue Type | Description | Response Time |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| Critical | System down, major outage | 2–4 hours |
| High | Feature broken, impacting business workflow | 8 hours |
| Medium | Minor bug or error | 24 hours |
| Low | UI fixes, general questions | 48–72 hours |

---

# Maintenance Window
Regular updates occur during low-traffic hours. Clients are informed before deployment.

---

# Change Requests
- Scoped & documented  
- Estimated timeline provided  
- Implemented after client approval
