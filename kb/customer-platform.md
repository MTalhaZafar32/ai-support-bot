# Customer Platforms

AminQa builds scalable, secure platforms for customer onboarding, account management, and digital service delivery.

### ✅ Portal Development
- Customer dashboards
- Self-service portals
- Account management systems

### ✅ Authentication & Access Control
- Secure login/registration
- Role-based access
- OAuth2, JWT, SSO support

### ✅ Integrations
- Payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal)
- CRMs & automation systems
- Email/SMS providers

### ✅ Multi-tenant Architecture
- For SaaS products
- Isolated environments
- Centralized admin panel
